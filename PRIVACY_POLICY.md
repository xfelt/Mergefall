# Privacy Policy — Mergefall (Merge Survivor: Board Quest)

**Effective date:** 2026-06-11
**Developer:** Xfeltech
**Contact:** aachergui1@gmail.com

This privacy policy describes how the Android game **Mergefall** ("the app", package
`com.xfeltech.mergefall`) handles your information. Host this document at a public URL
(e.g. GitHub Pages or your website) and link that URL in Google Play Console →
Store listing → Privacy policy.

---

## 1. Data we collect

### Data stored only on your device

The app saves your game progress (board state, currencies, upgrades, collection,
daily-reward streak, settings) **locally on your device** using Android app storage.
This data never leaves your device unless a third-party service below is enabled,
and it is deleted when you uninstall the app or clear its storage.

The app does not require an account, login, or any personal information to play.

### Data collected by third-party services

When the published build includes the following SDKs, they may collect data as
described by their own privacy policies:

| Service | Purpose | Data involved | Privacy policy |
|---|---|---|---|
| Google AdMob | Rewarded and interstitial ads | Advertising ID, IP address, device info, coarse location, ad interaction data | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Google Play Billing | In-app purchases | Purchase tokens and order information (processed by Google Play; we never see your payment details) | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Firebase Analytics | Anonymous usage statistics (e.g. level completion, session length) | App instance ID, device info, coarse usage events | https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy |
| Firebase Remote Config | Game balance tuning | App instance ID, configuration fetches | https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy |

We do **not** collect names, email addresses, contacts, photos, precise location,
microphone or camera data.

## 2. How data is used

- **Ads:** AdMob serves rewarded ads (optional, watched in exchange for in-game
  rewards) and interstitial ads. Ad data is used to serve and measure ads.
- **Purchases:** Google Play processes in-app purchases of in-game currency.
- **Analytics:** Aggregated, anonymous gameplay statistics help us balance and
  improve the game.
- We do not sell personal data.

## 3. Advertising ID & personalized ads

The app's advertising integrations use the Android Advertising ID. You can reset
your Advertising ID or opt out of ads personalization at any time in Android
**Settings → Google → Ads**. Where required (EEA/UK), a consent prompt is shown
before personalized ads are served.

## 4. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal
information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through
a third-party SDK, contact us and we will work with the provider to delete it.

## 5. In-app purchases and loot boxes

The app offers optional in-app purchases of virtual currency and a "Lucky Chest"
random-reward mechanic. Drop-rate odds are disclosed in-game before purchase.
Virtual items have no real-world value and are not refundable except as required
by law or Google Play policy.

## 6. Data retention & deletion

- Local save data: stored until you uninstall the app or clear its storage.
- Third-party data: retained per the policies of Google AdMob / Firebase. You can
  request deletion of analytics data tied to your app instance by contacting us.

## 7. Your rights

Depending on your region (e.g. GDPR in the EEA/UK, CCPA in California), you may have
rights to access, correct, or delete personal data, and to object to or restrict its
processing. To exercise these rights, email **aachergui1@gmail.com**. We respond
within 30 days.

## 8. Security

Game data is stored in the app's private storage sandbox. Network communication with
Google services uses encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

## 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves (for example, when an SDK is added or
removed). Material changes will be reflected by a new effective date at the top of
this document and noted in the app's store listing release notes.
