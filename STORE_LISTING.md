## Full description (max 4000 characters)

**Draft (use as-is or trim to fit; current length noted at end):**

Merge Survivor: Board Quest is a board-game style idle merge RPG. Combine units on the board, fight off waves of enemies, and grow stronger through permanent meta progression.

**How it works**

Place and merge units on a tactical board. Match identical units to create stronger ones. Waves of enemies attack—your merged army fights back automatically. Win battles to earn gold and resources, then spend them on meta upgrades that make every run easier. Unlock new units, boards, and abilities as you progress.

**Why players love it**

- **Satisfying merge gameplay** — Drag, drop, and merge. Every merge feels impactful and feeds into your strategy.
- **Board-game tactics** — Positioning and placement matter. The board is your battlefield.
- **Fight waves** — Enemies keep coming. Your merged units handle the action while you focus on the next merge.
- **Meta progression** — Permanent upgrades, unlocks, and progression that carry over between runs so you always move forward.
- **Idle-friendly** — Progress even when you’re away; come back to rewards and new goals.

Merge Survivor: Board Quest is for fans of merge games, survivor-style combat, and board-game strategy. Download now and conquer the board.
